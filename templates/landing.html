About the Project

The Spotify Song Popularity Prediction System leverages machine learning techniques to classify songs as "Popular" or "Not Popular" based on their audio features. This system provides data-driven insights for artists, producers, record labels, and streaming platforms to understand what makes a song successful.

Our Mission

Our mission is to develop an accurate machine learning model that can predict the popularity of a song based on its audio features. This tool is designed to help artists, producers, record labels, and streaming platforms make data-driven decisions about music production, marketing, and audience engagement. We aim to enhance the music industry's ability to forecast trends and optimize strategies for song success.

Our Vision

Our long-term vision is to revolutionize the music industry by integrating our platform with streaming services like Spotify, Apple Music, and YouTube Music, providing real-time insights into song popularity. This will help professionals in the music industry make informed decisions and optimize their content for maximum impact. We strive to continuously improve our model and offer valuable insights to artists, producers, and labels globally.
